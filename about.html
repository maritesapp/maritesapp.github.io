About MaritesAI

  • Ano ba 'tong MaritesAI?

What is MaritesAI?


MaritesAI is a hilarious camera roast app that uses AI to analyze your photo and generate funny, unsolicited roasts—parang tsismis lang!

Unlike social media platforms where you post content for others to see, MaritesAI is all about you. Just take a selfie, let our AI spot funny details, and get roasted in authentic Filipino voice.

It's not about connecting with friends or sharing memes. It's a quick, fun experience that gives you a good laugh at your own expense—walang personalan, lahat biro lang!

So if you're ready for some light‑hearted, AI‑powered tsismis, MaritesAI is here to deliver.

Made for Filipinos, by Filipinos


MaritesAI is built with the Filipino sense of humor in mind. We know that our humor is unique—mix of tsismis, asar, and lambing—and that's exactly what you'll get.

The roasts are delivered in authentic Filipino voice, using Tagalog and sometimes beki language, so it feels like you're being roasted by your own tita or beshie. Walang halong foreign accent—pure Pinoy fun!



Why MaritesAI?


In a world full of serious social media, political noise, and endless scrolling, sometimes you just need a good laugh—at yourself!

MaritesAI is our answer to that need. It's not about solving big problems; it's about giving you a moment of pure, light‑hearted fun. Just a simple account, no feeds, no drama. Just you, your camera, and a hilarious roast that feels like tsismis from your favorite tita.

Quick, hassle‑free fun


Sign up once and you're good to go! We give you a daily limit of free roasts—perfect for a quick laugh. Want more? Upgrade to remove the limit and roast as much as you want.

No complicated steps, no need to follow anyone. Just snap a photo, get roasted, laugh, and share the fun!

Good vibes only


We keep it positive and playful. The roasts are witty and slightly mean, but never hateful or offensive. It's all in the spirit of Filipino asar—where the teasing is a sign of affection.

No hidden agendas


We're not here to sell your data or push an agenda. We're just here to make you smile (or maybe cringe a little). That's the whole point.

How do we keep MaritesAI free?


MaritesAI is free to use, and we plan to keep it that way. To cover the costs of AI APIs and hosting, we offer a free tier with daily roast limits—so you can enjoy a few hilarious roasts every day without paying a single peso.

If you want unlimited roasts and extra features, you can upgrade to a premium plan. That's how we keep the lights on while still giving everyone a taste of the fun.

No hidden fees, no selling your data. Just pure, hilarious roasts—with the option to upgrade if you get hooked!
