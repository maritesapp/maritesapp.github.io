How is it different from other Social Media platforms?


People use Facebook to stay connected with friends and family, to discover what's going on in the world, and to share and express what matters to them.

Marites app, in contrary, is built with anonymity in mind. It's a place where you can post things that are "too risky to share". Either because it is considered taboo or the issue is something you dont want to be attached with your public image. Although you can Follow a certain account to see their posts, its not really meant to connect you with your close family and friends.

What it intends to do is to allow one stranger to post some funny content, then allow bunch of other strangers to share laugher with you.

It is basicallly a Meme app.

Specially crafted for Filipino audience


According to the recent studies. Facebook had 86.75 million users in the Philippines in early 2024. This app is specially tailored for these users. Facebook is great, but since it used by too many cultures around the world, it fails to capture Filipino-specific needs. That is why you will notice in the app and website, it is littered with Tagalog and sometimes beki language, to make Filipinos feel at home.



What are problems we are trying to address?



Privacy of your Personal Data


Filipinos should be able to consume and publish contents without sacrificing their privacy. We strongly discourage providing your real information when signing up an account, posting your whereabouts, your family photos etc. This is a platform to post funny videos of cats, memes and jokes. Generally anything that is not too personal.

Other platforms is going through the process of Enshittification


Enshittification, also known as crapification and platform decay, is the term used to describe the pattern in which online products and services decline in quality over time. Initially, vendors create high-quality offerings to attract users, then they degrade those offerings to better serve business customers, and finally degrade their services to users and business customers to maximize profits for shareholders.
This platform is a fresh take, free from the toxic stuffs going on in the bureaucracy of large social media platforms like Meta or X. We dont really have shareholders to kiss ass, so pretty much everything you would like us to have in this app, we can give it to you.

What this app is not


This app is NOT a news app, everything you might find here is most likely a joke and not meant to be taken seriously.

Just Fun and good vibes


Politics, activism, hatred and emotionally negative posts has no place in this app.
If you want to debate with someone else about Politics, go to X.
If you are resentful with your family, wife, husband, friends goverment and so on. Post that shit in Facebook.
If you want to post your food or half-naked photos, post that shit in Instagram or TikTok
This app is for funny contents, not for other shit.
